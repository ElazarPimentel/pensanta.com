Standard website test sizes
Device class Viewport size Orientation Why check it
Phone small 360 × 640 Portrait Minimum modern mobile width; catches cramped navigation and long words.
Phone standard 390 × 844 Portrait Common iPhone-class viewport for primary mobile layout checks.
Phone large 430 × 932 Portrait Large phones; verifies hero height, sticky elements, and tap targets.
Tablet 768 × 1024 Portrait Classic tablet breakpoint; catches mobile-to-tablet layout transitions.
Tablet wide 1024 × 768 Landscape Tablet landscape and small desktop overlap.
Notebook 1366 × 768 Landscape Common laptop size; validates above-the-fold content on short screens.
Notebook large 1440 × 900 Landscape MacBook-style workspace; checks max-widths and spacing.
Desktop 1920 × 1080 Landscape Standard full HD monitor; baseline desktop review size.
Big monitor 2560 × 1440 Landscape QHD displays; catches content that stretches too wide.
Ultrawide 3440 × 1440 Landscape Wide monitor layout; verifies backgrounds and centered content.
Large TV 3840 × 2160 Landscape 4K displays and signage; checks scaling, line length, and media quality.
