Web Development, Enterprise Applications & Cybersecurity in Buenos Aires, Argentina

At Pensanta, a consultancy based in Buenos Aires, we develop enterprise web applications, professional corporate websites, manage projects with PMI and ART methodologies, and provide cybersecurity and technical training services. We combine technical expertise with proven methodologies to deliver concrete digital solutions.

What We Do

Web Applications

We develop scalable and maintainable web applications, from internal systems to enterprise platforms. We apply project management methodologies to ensure deliveries within agreed scope, budget, and timeline.

Websites

We design and build professional, optimized websites tailored to your business needs. From corporate sites to platforms with advanced functionalities.

Information Security

When companies face security incidents or need to strengthen their defenses, we step in quickly. We lead forensic recovery, strengthen defenses, and train teams to prevent future incidents.

Training

We train teams in Information Security, efficient use of technology tools, and practical application of AI tools for everyday office tasks: Excel optimization, document writing, automation of administrative processes, and other daily activities.

Our Approach

We apply project management methodologies according to the type of initiative: PMI for traditional projects and ART (Agile Release Train) for scaled agile initiatives. Our experience covers software development, security, infrastructure, and technical training, allowing us to deliver real, measurable results.
